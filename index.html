Why Barcelona?

Barcelona, the city of art and sea

Legend has it that Barcelona was founded by Hercules, long before Rome itself. But in recorded history, it was the Carthaginians and Romans who shaped the city, laying the foundations for what would become a vibrant Mediterranean capital. In the centuries that followed, Barcelona grew into a cultural beacon, known for its unique blend of gothic and modernist architecture.

Today, you can still feel that legacy. Among the bustling boulevards and tree-lined avenues, you’ll find Gaudí’s dreamlike creations, markets bursting with color, and local cafes offering pa amb tomàquet and other Catalan delights. Walk through the city's neighborhoods, climb up to Montjuïc or Park Güell, and take in sweeping views of the sea, the skyline, and the warm, golden light that defines this unforgettable city.

Whether you're chasing history, cuisine, beaches, or just the sun, Barcelona welcomes you with open arms.
